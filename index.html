DIGITAL FOR EDUCATION INDUSTRY

At Rosemary Interactive, we believe that education needs to be pervasive across the globe. The education sector of India is divided into two prime segments; the core segment comprises of schools and higher education, while the non-core comprises of coaching classes, pre-schools and vocational trainings.

The spread of technology in education is undeniable. Schools and colleges are digitizing classroom learning, transforming learning, making the limitless possibilities of technology possible in the classroom. Digital revolution creates immediate visibility into student engagement and many platforms allow both students and parents to receive instant information & feedback, rather than wait for a looming stack of papers, brochures, leaflets, Cds to be graded.
